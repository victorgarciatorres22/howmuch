HOW DOES IT WORK?

Have you ever wondered for how much you could sell your old camera, your jacket that you no longer wear, or your car? Just take some pictures of the item you want to sell and we tell you the price! "How much" analyses the brand, the condition, the year it was made and other factors; it compares it to a huge database, and it gives you a highly precise value of the item you intend to sell.
